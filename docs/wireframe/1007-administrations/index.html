Recommendation: Option 2 — Timeline

The Timeline best serves the core goal of clear term management and readable history. Because the feature is fundamentally about periods that succeed one another — a new administration auto-ends the previous one — a chronological spine communicates that model more directly than any list or form. The current term is highlighted at the top for day-to-day management, while every past term (with its end date and roster) is one click away, making the preserved history genuinely browsable. Option 5 (Split View) is the strongest alternative when editing older terms is frequent, and Option 4 (Assignment Wizard) pairs naturally with the Timeline as the flow triggered by "+ New administration." Option 1 remains the lowest-risk baseline that ships fastest on the current design system.

★ Recommendation: Option 2 — Card Grid

The flow is optimized for two things at once: clear community creation and easy switching. The Card Grid is the only option that serves both in a single, familiar hub — a grid of the communities you belong to (with role badges and a clear "Current" marker) alongside an always-present create card. Switching is a single click and creation is never more than one card away, while the layout reuses the standard index/card pattern already in the design system. Option 3 (Wizard) is the strongest pure-creation experience and pairs well as the destination behind the "+ Create" card; Option 5 (Onboarding Hub) is the ideal first-run entry point before any community exists; and Option 4 (Command Switcher) is a valuable keyboard accelerator to layer on later for power users.

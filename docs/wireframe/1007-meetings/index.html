UX Notes

  • 1 Meetings live under the existing sidebar-shell — one nav item, no new mental model. Upcoming and Past are simply two labelled groups in the same list.

  • 1 The meeting list never leaves the screen — the secretary can jump between meetings without losing the record they're editing.

The roster-checklist as hero: a full-width, fast present/absent list for one meeting. Every member is a row with a Present toggle, a sticky "X present of Y" running summary and a mark-all control — built for taking attendance in seconds during a live gathering.

★ Recommended: Option 3 — Split View

It keeps the meeting list in context while the secretary records attendance and writes minutes in one place. Attendance and Minutes render side by side, so both primary jobs — fast attendance taking and easy minutes writing — are completed in a single pass with one Save. Option 4 (Attendance Grid) is the stronger choice if attendance-only speed during a live gathering is the top priority, and Options 2 and 5 are excellent complementary member-facing views for browsing meetings and reading minutes.
